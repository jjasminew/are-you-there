our mission

today, more than 40% of teenagers in the united states say they struggle with persistent feelings of sadness or hopelessness. about 1 in 5 students consider suicide and about 1 in 10 attempt suicide. if left unaddressed, the ongoing teen mental health crisis will leave many young human beings at high risk of losing the fight against depression.

"are you there?" was created to bring to attention the struggles of depression, anxiety, and suicidal thoughts, and to recreate the experience of using technology to comfort those who are struggling. our mission is to highlight the importance of teen mental health in the context of an increasingly digitized world and to walk players through the process of comforting a friend over the phone.
